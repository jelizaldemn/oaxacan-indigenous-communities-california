Indigenous Peoples of Oaxaca in California

Oaxacan Indigenous Communities in California

Approximately 350,000 people of Oaxacan Indigenous origin live in California, speaking more than 15 Indigenous languages. This visualization shows the distribution by language, region of origin in Oaxaca, and destination region in California.

Data Sources

Data compiled from the Indigenous Farmworker Study (IFS), National Center for Farmworker Health (NCFH), U.S. Census 2000/2020, MICOP, Fresno Bee, and academic studies from UC Berkeley and UC Santa Cruz. Figures represent estimates based on multiple sources, given significant undercounting in official censuses.

About This Community

Oaxacan Indigenous communities represent the fastest-growing farmworker population in California. Many speak only their Indigenous language, without Spanish or English, creating unique communication barriers and obstacles to accessing services.
